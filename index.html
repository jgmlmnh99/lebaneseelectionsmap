Lebanon — Electoral Map

Hello, if you are here then you are probably interested in the fascinating world of elections as well as the fascinating world of maps. This is not a tool sanctioned by any political party or governmental entity, it is simply a passion project of one person. It came as a result of the frustration that politics and understanding elections in Lebanon is often not presented in a simple and understandable way to all.

This is a simple tool used to visualize election results in Lebanon. Enjoy filling in your predictions of the Lebanese Parliamentary elections and seeing it come to life on an interactive map.

If you notice any mistakes or have any remarks or recommendations for our website, please send an email to lebaneseelectionsmap@gmail.com

Enjoy Mapping!
